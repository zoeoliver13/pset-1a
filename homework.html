Welcome to the 2000s Nostalgia Page!

What is it?

This page reminds kids born from 2000-2004 of our best childhood memories before we entered the stressful world of highschool and began preparing for college. It doesn't really do anything, besides shows pictures from things we used to do, watch, and listen to, but its nice to reflect upon and see how old we've gotten. Here, you will be able to find our favorite shows, toys, games, and music from when we were little kids.
