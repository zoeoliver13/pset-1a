TV Shows

A lot of our best childhood memories came from watching tv! All the way from Blues Clues and the Backyardagains to Pokemon and Drake & Josh; Including those nights staying up late and watching Family Guy, or Full House. Even randomly waking up at like 3am to George Lopez playing. Do any of these, cartoons bring back old childhood memories of sitting down infront of the tv with a bowl of cereal?

Nickelodeon VS Cartoon Network

One internal conflict we all had, and have still not resolved, was trying to decide who had the better cartoons?? In my opinion, it all boiled down to Cartoon Network and Nickelodeon. How do they expect us to choose between Code Name: Kids Next Door and Danny Phantom?

SitComs

As we got a bit older, a lot of us transitioned from mostly watching cartoons to sit coms. Personally, I think that Disney Channel took the crown for having the best sitcoms, but Nickelodean had some bangers too; like Drake & Josh and ICarly.
